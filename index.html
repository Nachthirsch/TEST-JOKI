Our Story

MonicaWorld was born out of a deep concern for the environment and a determination to promote sustainable agriculture. Starting as a small initiative in Bekasi, we have grown into a movement—transforming organic waste into fertilizer that nurtures life through a natural fermentation process.

Our factory has been operating since 2019, with a vision to produce high-quality organic fertilizer from natural ingredients such as goat manure, cocopeat, and volcanic soil. For several years, we have focused on perfecting our production process and maintaining consistent product quality.

Now, in 2025, we are taking a big step to expand our reach internationally. MonicaWorld has begun exporting to several countries, including Japan, China, Switzerland, Australia, Malaysia, and Singapore.

As a growing local business, we are committed to providing environmentally friendly agricultural solutions, enriching the soil, and supporting farmers in achieving better harvests naturally. We believe the future of agriculture lies in harmony between people, nature, and the land.

With a production capacity of 20 tons per month, MonicaWorld is proud to prove that local innovation can grow and bring benefits to the world—from our soil, for your harvest.
